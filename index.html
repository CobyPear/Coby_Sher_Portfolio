About Me

Full-stack web developer with a background in music composition, production, and education. Recently earned a full stack web development certificate from the University of Minnesota. Skills include JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node, React, Express, SASS, Core Java, OOP, and Agile methodology. Focused on mobile responsive and accessible web design.
I'm interested in developing unique experiences on the web. Recently I worked with a team of three to develop an electronic logbook for pilots to keep track of their flights and generate charts for their total hours logged.
I love learning new things and coding has been an amazing outlet to do just that. The overwhelming amount of technology and resources keep me interested and coming back for more knowledge day after day! When I'm not learning new things about coding, I enjoy spending time with my partner and our pets, making electronic music, and playing games like Magic: the Gathering.
